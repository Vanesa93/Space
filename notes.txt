Space gameplay:

The game is for one player:You play with a space ship and you have 3 lifes. The game has unlimited count of levels. 
From the right side of the screen are coming treasure chests and meteoroids.

The goal of the game is collecting maximum count of treasure chests and keep avoiding meteoroites.
In the first level the player should collect 20 treasure boxes to pass. With every level
the amount of the treasures to be collected increases with 10 and the speed of the 
treasure boxes and the meteoroids increases with 1.  If the player collides with a meteoroite,
he loses 1 life. The game over, when the player collides with meteorites 3 times. 

The player can pause and restart the game.
